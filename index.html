Primal Design

Sketch like crazy. Fill up pages with your ideas. They do not have to be super detailed or even understandable to anyone else. Put every idea you can onto paper.
See Jørn Utzon's sketches here!














If you get stuck or need inspiration, walk around, and look at everyone else’s work to help jump start your brain.





















Make a mess. Have fun with whatever you are designing. Get your hands dirty. Read a manifesto on our open mind.


















































Don’t clean off your desk or your document. Your next inspiration could be sitting right there in front of you. Read An Incomplete Manifesto For Growth, # 25.




















Most importantly, make what you want to make. As you can see, I did this with these illustrations. I love dinosaurs and architecture. I combined the two and made these. Do what you enjoy because you are going to enjoy the process and be proud of what you did. Try to include what you enjoy in whatever you do.
